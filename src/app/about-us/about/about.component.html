Hope Apostolic Children Ministry Profile

Hope Apostolic Children Ministries ( HACM). Started on 31st- March- 2019. Definition: HACM is a Christian children organisation that works with all churches, organisations, Companies and individuals that believe in the Bible ( written word of God).

Vision
To see children become disciples of Jesus Christ and filled with Hope to transform their Lives, Families, Communities and Nations around the globe.

Mission
To Nurture children to the full stature of Christ

Mission statement
Nurturing children holistically through the 3H ( Heart, Head & Hand ) approach.

Ministry scripture
Colossians 1: 27- 28...Christ in you, the Hope of Glory. ...we proclaim Him, admonishing everyone and teaching everyone with all wisdom, so that we may present everyone complete in Christ.

Definition
A child is a spiritual being in a human body of less than 18 years. Classes:

  • FLOWERS ( 0- 4 ) years
  • STARS ( 5- 9) years
  • CHAMPIONS ( 10- 13 ) years
  • GIANTS ( 14- 17 ) years
